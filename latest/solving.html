Solving the Model

The gensys routine

The DSGE model is written down in its canonical representation:

Γ0 s_t = Γ1 s_{t-1} + C + Ψ ε_t + Π η_t

where Γ0, Γ1, C, Ψ, and Π are matrices of coefficients for s_t (states at time t), s_{t-1} (lagged states), ε_t (exogenous shocks) and η_t (expectational shocks).

DSGE.jl solves the model to obtain its state-space form using the gensys routine of Chris Sims, introduced in this paper. We provide a standalone native Julia implementation of the routine (gensys) as well as a wrapper for AbstractModel subtypes (solve):

When the Gensys.jl package becomes ready for use, we intend to deprecate our gensys code and substitute the gensysdt method for our code.
